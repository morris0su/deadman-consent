5. SMS Notifications and Consent

5.1 Express Consent to SMS

By enabling monitoring in Deadman Alert and designating an emergency contact, you provide your express written consent for us to send SMS text messages to your emergency contact when you've been inactive for your specified threshold period.

5.2 SMS Content

SMS messages sent to your emergency contact will include:

  • Your name
  • Duration of inactivity
  • Request for wellness check
  • Opt-out instructions (reply STOP)

5.3 Message Frequency

  • Maximum frequency: 1 SMS per 24-hour period per user
  • Messages are sent ONLY when inactivity threshold is exceeded
  • This is emergency-only messaging, NOT marketing or promotional

5.4 Opt-Out Rights

Your emergency contact can opt out of SMS alerts at any time by:

  • Replying STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT to any message
  • Opt-out requests are processed immediately
  • They can opt back in by replying START, SUBSCRIBE, or UNSTOP

Standard message and data rates may apply. These charges are determined by your emergency contact's mobile carrier.

5.6 Consent Withdrawal

You can withdraw your consent to SMS notifications at any time by:

  • Deleting your account
