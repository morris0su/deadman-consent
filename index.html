How It Works

1

Download & Sign In

Download Deadman Alert from the iOS App Store (coming soon - currently in TestFlight beta). Sign in securely with Apple.

2

Designate Emergency Contact

Choose one trusted person to receive alerts. Enter their phone number and confirm you understand they'll receive SMS notifications.

3

Enable Monitoring

Activate monitoring with a single tap. The app begins tracking your device activity in the background.

4

Stay Active

Use your phone normally. Every time you interact with your device, your "last seen" timestamp updates automatically.

5

Automatic Alerts

If 24 hours pass without activity, your emergency contact receives an SMS asking them to check on you. That's it!

About Deadman Alert

Deadman Alert was created to provide peace of mind for people living alone, elderly individuals, remote workers, and anyone concerned about being unable to call for help in an emergency.

Similar to medical alert systems like Life Alert, but leveraging the smartphone you already carry, Deadman Alert provides affordable, automatic emergency monitoring without expensive hardware or monthly monitoring fees.

We believe everyone deserves safety and peace of mind, which is why we've built a simple, privacy-focused solution that just works.
